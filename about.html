About Me

I'm a passionate web developer with a strong focus on creating intuitive and engaging user experiences. With a background in both design and development, I bring a unique perspective to every project I work on.

My journey in tech began when I built my first website at 15, and since then I've been hooked on the endless possibilities of the web. I specialize in front-end development but enjoy working across the full stack to bring complete solutions to life.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking in the mountains, reading sci-fi novels, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I believe in continuous learning and pushing boundaries to create digital experiences that make a difference.

I approach every project with enthusiasm and attention to detail, ensuring that the final product not only meets but exceeds expectations. My goal is to build websites and applications that are not just functional, but delightful to use.
